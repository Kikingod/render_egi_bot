
About BearUp:

BearUp was founded in 2018 with a mission to create high-quality, durable gear for outdoor adventurers. 
Our goal is to provide hikers, campers, and explorers with reliable equipment to enhance their outdoor experiences.

We are passionate about sustainability and all our products are made with eco-friendly materials. 
We believe that the beauty of nature should be preserved for future generations, and we take steps to minimize our environmental impact.

Based in Colorado, BearUp has grown into a trusted brand within the outdoor community. 
From the rocky mountains to the dense forests, BearUp gear is built to withstand the elements and keep you moving forward.
